About Us

Our in-house chefs take sustainably-sourced fruits plus vegetables and turn them into craveworthy eats. Then, we pass all that goodness onto you. We got you. 24/7.

FreshGo is on a mission to make it really easy to eat more fruits plus vegetables every day. From seed to plate, we’re committed to a better food system, one that prioritizes human and planetary health. We are transforming what we eat, what we grow, and how we grow it — one crop (and box) at a time. Our chef-crafted food is delicious, easy to prep, and built on sustainably sourced fruits plus veggies.
